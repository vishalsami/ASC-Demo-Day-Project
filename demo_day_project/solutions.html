There are many solutions people have come up with over the days to stop waste pollution. The easiest solution is known as recycling.

What does it mean to recycle? To recycle is to convert waste materials into reusable materials and objects. Instead of throwing a plastic bottle on the ground and leaving it, put it in the recycling bin so that people can make it reusable

Why Recycle?

  1. You would be helping keep your city clean
  2. Recycling helps reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators.
  3. Recycling conserves natural resources such as timber, water and minerals.
